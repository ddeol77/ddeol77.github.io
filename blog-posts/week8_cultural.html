Cultural Blog: Dealing with Conflict

    Conflict

  • The last time I had conflict with someone was at work. We had a disagreement about how to manage a client. My co-worker wanted to do everything the client asked but I insisted we not say yes to everything. Ultimately we agreed to disagree.
  • The source of the conflict was a difference in philosophies and beliefs.
  • I tried to be respectful and explain my position without being offensive or rude.
  • I think since the other person would not budge the conflict grew more instense initially. After some more thought I realized the reasons why the other person had taken the position they had. After that realization I was able to understand them better and the conflict died down.
  • I think looking back now I would try to understand the other persons reasons for the position they were taking sooner
  • I learned that everyone has their reasons for why the act and feel the way they do. Once we realize that it's easier to understand people.
