Cultural Blog: Pairing and Giving Feedback

    Pairing

  • I really enjoy pairing with others to solve the challenges. Two heads are better than one and someone else can always point something out that you overlooked or a mistake you may have made. I think it's rewarding when you solve a problem as a team and build comradery. The only thing that can be frustrating or difficult would be if there is a problem with the communication in the team.

    • Feedback

  • I'm usually appreciative of feedback when it's given. I'm always trying to get better and I feel that constructive feedback is essential for that. Going forward I will speak up more if the person that I'm pairing with is not understanding concepts or is slowing the team down. I enjoy writing feedback helping someone else improve and get better. I think feedback and pairing can help people understand concepts a lot quicker compared to just trying to tackle something alone.
