Cultural Blog: Stereotype Threat

    The Stereotype Threat

  • I haven't personally felt the stereotype threat but can see how this may effect other groups of people. I can see how a negative self-image can cause someone to not perform to their maximum potential and cause them to underachieve.
  • I think that we can try to educate minority groups who have this issue and help address the problem. We would need to present this information at schools that have a high percentage of minorities and help create a positive self-image for them. Once we help them identify this condition they can start to create strategies to overcome it.
