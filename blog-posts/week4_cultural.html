    Technology and Security

  • An issue that you see in the news often these days is security. Technology is being used for everything these days and that opens up the possiblity for breaches. As everyone becomes more connected they also become more vulnerable. The hackings of Target, Neiman Marcus, and even Android users has made everyone recognize this as a real threat.
  • A solution that could help would be to fight technology with technology. We've seen the implementation of higher tech security help safegaurd information. Another solution is to adopt security chips in credit cards. This is something that is commonly done throughout Europe. At the end of the day recognizing the potential threat of security breaches and investing time an money towards prevention is the best thing an individual or organization can do.
