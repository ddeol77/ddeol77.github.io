My Thinking Style and Learning

    My thinking style - Concrete Random...i think...maybe?

  • I had a hard time categorizing myself into one of the 4 Gregorc Thinking Styles. I know that I do really well with hands on project work especially when it's team based. I struggle when I have to sit for long periods of time and read material. I think the way DBC is setup is great for someone like me.

    • My learning experience in Phase 0 so far

  • The biggest struggle for me has been making time to get the assignments completed. I also have a hard time focusing on reading material for a long period of time. I know everyone has other comittiments and it's not an excuse but working full-time and having a family does make it challenging to put in 15-25 hours a week towards these challenges. Fortunately I was able to work something out with my employer and am taking a day off once a week in order to complete the Phase 0 assignments. I also realized that I'm much more productive at my local library than at home.

    • Growth vs. Fixed mindset

  • I think that by having a combination of independent study, group activities, and projects that have creative input will help expand the ways we learn and think. Personally I think that working on my weakness during the Phase 0 portion will help me.
